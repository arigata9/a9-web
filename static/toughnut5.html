This is a small pictured documentation and resource about how I modified my 2005 PowerMac G5.

This was a little project during my school time. I wanted to keep the main aspects of the aluminum case and its functionalities (like the power button, drive cage, DVD drive) but convert it to a mATX tray. That way it could hold a motherboard including an AiO watercooling solution.

These pictures may or may not be ordered by the time they were taken, but they resemble each step pretty good.
